About CCMADS


The Communication, Culture, and Media Association of Doctoral Students (CCMADS) serves to enhance the Communication, Culture, & Media program both academically and socially by supplementing it with learning and networking opportunities as well as extra-curricular activities. The association provides a forum for CCM graduate students to voice their opinions and have them represented by the CCMADS Board to the Graduate Student Association.

We seek to inspire leadership, collegiality, and departmental growth. As a group, we encourage and embrace the principles surrounding interdisciplinary thought and action. To do so, we facilitate exchange and cooperation among all doctoral students in the social sciences and humanities at Drexel. We believe that media and communication are fundamentally intertwined with culture and need to be studied in order to understand the impact they have in society. We believe in preparing CCM Doctoral students to be successful leaders in academia, politics, and corporate industry. We believe in advancing the study of Communication, Culture, & Media.
